About This Majestic Creature

“Woof, woof, woof. *panting*"

Takoyaki, The Goodest Doge

Takoyaki is a shiba inu. You might be familiar with shibas as the "doge" meme all over the internet. They are the smallest of the Japanese spitz dogs, but they have big personalities. Seriously, it's too much to handle sometimes. You'll know what that means when you witness a dog throwing a tantrum after being told "no". Must be what having a teenager is like.
Tako was born September 3rd, 2017. Being born and raised in Vancouver,BC, he's been spoiled with beautiful hiking trails. Despite loving the great outdoors, he hates walking through puddles or mud. His favorite treats are beef liver, and he adores his pet cat, Hyo.
